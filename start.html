Getting started

Requirements:

  • A card reader capable of reading/writing SD cards.

Installing:

  1. Download the latest packaged release from the downloads page and extract it.
  2. Insert an SD card in your card reader.
  3. cd into the directory you just extracted.
  4. Run ./setup.sh /dev/X where X is the device of your SD card.

You can find out what X is by running dmesg | tail after having inserted the card.

You probably need root privileges for running setup.sh.

Running:

  1. Insert the SD card in the Beagle Board or Gumstix (the device).
  2. Connect your PC to the serial port on your device and open it with your terminal program of choice.
  3. Power your device on, either via USB or a power adapter.
  4. Log in as root when the login: prompt shows, no password is needed.
